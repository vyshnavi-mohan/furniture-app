About Elegant Interiors


Tradition since 1889

Over the past 10 years, HomeTown has been bringing the latest designs & fashion to Indian homes. HomeTown offers the widest and best in class range in furniture, home furnishings & decor, modular kitchens, home improvement and more. Our commitment to quality and timeless designs has helped us evolve over the years and it indeed fills us with pride to be the first choice of many.

Being recognized as India’s biggest store in homemaking, renovation and decor, our products are exclusively designed while keeping durability and comfort at priority. From classy sofas to chic dining sets to kitchen essentials and artifacts, we offer everything to beautify your living space. Since 2006, we’ve grown to over 40 stores across 22 cities. Keeping pace with the tech-savvy world, we have maintained a strong web presence with prompt online services.
